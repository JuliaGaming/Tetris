## Running the game
From this directory, run:
```console
julia tetris.jl
```

Your highscore will be save to the `.highscore` file.


## Controls (wasd + qe)
* `a` and `d` for left and right movement
* `s` to move down
* `w` to snap down
* `q` and `e` for left and right rotations
* `r` to restart
* `esc` to quit
